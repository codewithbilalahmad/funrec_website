Privacy Policy

Your privacy is important to us

Information We Collect

Welcome to Funrec Audio Recorder App. This Privacy Policy explains what information the app accesses, how it is used, and the choices you have.

The app is designed to be simple and private. It collects no personal information by default. However, it requires the following permissions to function:

üé§

Microphone Access

Required to record audio when you start a recording session. Audio is processed locally and never transmitted externally.

üîî

Foreground Service

Shows a notification while recording in the background so you're always aware when recording is active and can easily stop it.

How We Use Information

We do not collect or share any personal data. The permissions are used only to:

  • Capture audio from your microphone when you start a recording
  • Show a persistent notification while recording in the background for transparency and control
  • Save recordings only inside the app's private file directory (internal storage)
  • Allow you to manage, playback, and export your recordings at your discretion

Recordings are not stored in public device storage or shared automatically. All audio processing happens locally on your device.

Sharing & Third-Party Services

The app does not share your recordings or personal data with any third parties. It does not include ads, third-party trackers, analytics, or any external data transmission.

Note: If you manually share a recording through your device's share options, the chosen destination app/service will receive the file ‚Äî this is beyond our control and subject to that service's privacy policy.

Data Security

Your recordings remain private in the app's internal storage directory. They are not accessible by other apps unless you explicitly choose to share them.

If you uninstall the app, your saved recordings may also be deleted unless you have exported them manually to external storage.

Children's Privacy

The app is for general audiences and does not knowingly collect information from children under 13. If you believe a child under 13 has provided us with personal information, please contact us immediately so we can take appropriate action.

Changes to this Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time to reflect changes in our practices or legal requirements. The "Last Updated" date at the bottom of this page will always reflect the latest version.

We encourage you to review this policy periodically to stay informed about how we protect your information.

Frequently Asked Questions

Find answers to common questions about FunRec

How does background recording work? +

FunRec uses Android's foreground service to continue recording even when the app is in the background or the screen is off. This ensures your recordings continue uninterrupted while you use other apps.

What audio formats does FunRec support? +

FunRec records in high-quality MP3 format by default, balancing audio quality with file size. You can adjust the recording quality in settings to suit your needs.

Can I share my recordings? +

Yes! FunRec makes it easy to share your recordings via email, messaging apps, or cloud storage. Simply select the recording you want to share and use the share button.

How do mood filters work? +

Mood filters are tags you can assign to your recordings to organize them by context or emotion. For example, you can tag a recording as "Meeting", "Idea", or "Music" for easy filtering later.

Is FunRec free to use? +

FunRec is free to download and use with basic features. A premium version with advanced features like cloud backup and unlimited recording time is available as an in-app purchase.
